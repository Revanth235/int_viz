WMATA Ridership

This page provides a comprehensive analysis of DC Metro and Bus riders from 2019 to 2024. This is done with the help of interactive visualizations done through various tools. The dataset was obtained from WMATA Ridership Portal Data Portal WMATA Ridership Data Portal. Specifically the data was extracted from the snapshot PDFs and converted into CSV for ease. The data provides numbers on the total metro riders, bus, and parking which were used for these visualizations.

The Metro Ridership from 2019 to 2024 visualisation shows the monthly DC Metro ridership during the weekdays, Saturday, and Sunday. The data ranges from July 2019 to June 2024.
